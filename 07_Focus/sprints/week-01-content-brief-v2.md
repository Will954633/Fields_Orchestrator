## Credibility Rules (from Product Agent review)

1. **Never cite a single valuation number as fact in a public post.** Use ranges: "comps suggest $1.85M to $2.1M" not "valued at $2,101,803."
2. **If a listing has no price guide, say "Expressions of Interest" — don't impute a price.**
3. **Always acknowledge what the data might be missing:** "There's always a reason for a gap."
4. **Link to exact property pages, not generic /for-sale.** Specific = credible.
5. **Don't mention ad performance as social proof.** "63 people clicked" means nothing to a stranger.
