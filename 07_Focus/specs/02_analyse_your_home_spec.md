# Conversion Spec: Analyse Your Home

## Goal
- Capture seller-intent leads from the dedicated seller page with one clear above-the-fold CTA.
- Target conversion rate: 5-8% from page visits to successful email submit.

## Source Note
- `context/focus/content_research_data.md` was not accessible in this workspace.
- This spec uses the available strategic-plan guidance, seller-surface product notes, and the page URL named in the request.

## Benchmark Rules Applied
- One offer only.
- One field only on the first step.
- 6th-grade reading level.
- Short hero copy above the fold.

## Recommended Default Variant
- **Headline:** Thinking about selling your home?
- **Subheading:** Get a clear plan for price, timing, and what to do next.
- **Button label:** Get my home analysis
- **Form fields:** Email
- **Success message:** Thanks. We got your request. Will from Fields Estate will email you about the next step within 24 hours.

## Placement
- The form sits in the hero, above the fold, as the main page action.
- Keep the email field and button visible without scrolling on desktop and mobile.
- Place proof, FAQ, and process content below the hero.
- Remove or downplay competing CTAs in the hero.

## UX Notes
- Email field placeholder: `Enter your email`
- Small trust line under the button: `No spam. Just your home analysis.`
- After submit, either:
  - show inline success state, or
  - redirect to a thank-you state that explains the next step.
- Collect address only after the email capture succeeds, either in reply flow or on the thank-you step.

## Copy Variants To Test

### Variant A: Seller timing
- **Headline:** Thinking about selling your home?
- **Subheading:** Get a clear plan for price, timing, and what to do next.
- **Button label:** Get my home analysis
- **Why test it:** Best match for broad seller intent and late-night research behavior.

### Variant B: Outcome-first
- **Headline:** Find out what your next move could be worth
- **Subheading:** We will help you think through price, timing, and whether selling now makes sense.
- **Button label:** Send my analysis
- **Why test it:** Speaks to seller upside without sounding like a hard appraisal pitch.

### Variant C: Lower-pressure
- **Headline:** Get a simple review of your home
- **Subheading:** See what to fix, when to sell, and what buyers may pay attention to.
- **Button label:** Email me the next step
- **Why test it:** Softer framing if direct selling language underperforms.

## Recommended Below-Fold Sections
- How it works: 3 short steps
- What you get: price view, timing view, prep view
- Why Fields: local data, property-by-property analysis, direct follow-up
- FAQ: 3 to 5 short questions max

## Guardrails
- Do not lead with "free appraisal" language.
- Do not ask for address, phone, suburb, timeline, or message on the first screen.
- Do not add a second CTA in the hero.
- Do not make the page longer than needed to create trust.

## Friday Review Metrics
- Primary: submit conversion rate from page visits
- Secondary: qualified reply rate, booked conversation rate, thank-you-page completion rate
