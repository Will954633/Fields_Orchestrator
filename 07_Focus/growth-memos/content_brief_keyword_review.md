## What the brief is missing

These are the highest-demand adjacent topics visible in the repo that are underrepresented in Week 1:

1. `Houses for sale` suburb pages as content hooks
   - Robina: 1,300/mo
   - Burleigh Waters: 590/mo
   - Varsity Lakes: 480/mo
   - Recommendation: at least one Week 1 or Week 2 post should directly promote the suburb-specific `/for-sale?suburb=...` pages.

2. A Varsity Lakes search-led post
   - The brief only uses Varsity Lakes as a comparison foil.
   - Missing a direct Varsity demand capture angle despite 480/mo on `houses for sale varsity lakes`.

3. Flood-intent content for Burleigh Waters
   - `does Burleigh Waters flood` is one of the clearest search-intent winners in the system.
   - This is stronger for SEO and lead capture than another generic opinion post.

4. Living-in / relocation content
   - `Living in Robina` is the highest-volume suburb-guide cluster in the strategy docs (332 tracked variants).
   - This is more of a YouTube / evergreen article opportunity than a Facebook-only post, but it is too important to ignore.
