# New Ad Creative Concepts

Date: 2026-03-30
Status: `CONTINUE`

Goal: stay inside the winning property-story format rather than inventing a new creative direction.

## Concept 1

**Property:** 10 Razorbill Street, Burleigh Waters  
**Data used:** 3 bed, 2 bath, listed around $1,650,000, internal valuation $2,101,803, gap about $451,803

**Headline:**  
This Burleigh Waters Home Is Listed About $450K Below the Comparable Sales

**Body copy:**  
We found one of the biggest pricing gaps in Burleigh Waters right now.  

10 Razorbill Street is being marketed around $1.65M. Our comparable-sales model values it at just over $2.10M.  

That does not automatically mean it is a bargain. It means the gap is big enough to investigate.  

3 beds. 2 baths. Burleigh Waters. Full data breakdown on the site.

**CTA:**  
Learn More

**Target URL:**  
`/for-sale?suburb=Burleigh%20Waters`

**Budget recommendation:**  
Start at A$15/day for 4 days. If LPV stays under A$0.35, move it to A$20/day by the end of the first week.

**Creative notes:**  
- Single-image or carousel
- Big overlay: `-$451,803 vs valuation`
- Secondary line: `Why is the gap this wide?`

## Concept 2

**Property:** 27 Seville Circuit, Burleigh Waters  
**Data used:** renovated 4 bed, pool, listed at $1,699,000, internal valuation $1,965,485, roughly $266K below comps

**Headline:**  
Renovated 4-Bed With Pool. Still $266K Under the Comps.

**Body copy:**  
This is the kind of property story that gets attention for the right reason.  

27 Seville Circuit has the features buyers want in Burleigh Waters: renovated 4-bed layout, pool, strong suburb demand.  

It is listed at $1,699,000. Our valuation model puts it closer to $1,965,485.  

So why is it cheaper than the next comp set? The answer is not the house. It is the exact position inside the suburb.

See the full analysis before the market catches up.

**CTA:**  
Learn More

**Target URL:**  
`/for-sale?suburb=Burleigh%20Waters`

**Budget recommendation:**  
Start at A$12/day for 4 days. Scale to A$15-A$18/day only if it beats A$0.40 LPV.

**Creative notes:**  
- Single image with hero photo + `Pool + Reno + Price Gap` overlay
- Hook line in image text: `So what's depressing the price?`

## Why these two

- Both are specific property stories, which is the format already winning in Meta.
- Both use real property data already referenced in the current brief.
- Both keep the Fields voice in the data/proof lane instead of generic advice.
- Both can route to Burleigh Waters proof inventory without needing a new landing page first.

## Execution note

If exact live property-page URLs are confirmed before launch, swap the suburb-level `/for-sale` URL for the property page. The concept itself should not change.
