# Facebook Posting Schedule Recommendation

Date: 2026-03-30
Status: `CONTINUE`

## Gold Coast recommendation

Use Gold Coast local time (`AEST`, UTC+10).

Best core windows to test first:

- Tuesday to Thursday: `8:00-10:00 a.m.`
- Tuesday to Thursday: `4:00-8:00 p.m.`
- Friday: `8:00-10:00 a.m.`
- Sunday: `9:00-10:00 a.m.` for lighter community-style content

Avoid making Saturday a major publishing day. If you post on Saturday, use it only for a low-stakes local/lifestyle post.

## Recommended weekly cadence

- 5 posts per week if Will can sustain the quality
- Minimum viable cadence: 4 posts per week
- Do not chase 7 days/week yet. The page is still small enough that consistency and format quality matter more than volume.

Recommended split:

- 2 short native videos
- 2 property-story image posts
- 1 opinion or article-promo post

## Format recommendation

Do not focus on text-only posts.

Primary focus:

- Short native video for data explainers and suburb pricing hooks
- Static image or carousel for property-story proof posts

Secondary:

- Text-led opinion prompts only when the goal is comments

Why:

- External Facebook benchmark work suggests images still have a slight engagement edge, but the format gap is small enough that topic quality matters more.
- The internal ad history is clearer than the platform-wide benchmark: proof-led property stories consistently outperform generic commentary.
- That means the best operating mix is not `video or text`; it is `video + proof-image`, with plain text used sparingly.

## Practical weekly schedule

Use this as the default starting calendar:

- Tuesday 8:30 a.m. — data video
- Tuesday 5:30 p.m. — property story
- Wednesday 8:30 a.m. — question/opinion or comparison post
- Thursday 5:30 p.m. — proof-led article promo or second data/video post
- Friday 8:30 a.m. — property story or market-data article promo

Optional:

- Sunday 9:30 a.m. — local/lifestyle post if Will has a genuine local photo

## What to measure for the first 2 weeks

- Video posts: 3-second views, 15-second views, shares
- Property-story posts: link clicks, LPV, comments
- Opinion posts: comments and saves

## Bottom line

For a small Gold Coast page, the right move is 4-5 quality posts per week, concentrated on Tuesday-Friday, with the biggest effort going into short video and proof-led property stories. Text-only should support the plan, not lead it.
