## Seller-Focused Deep Dives

These target specific seller anxieties — the questions people search for at 11pm when they're lying awake thinking about selling.

### "Have I Captured the Bulk of the Gain?"

**Target query:** "when to sell property", "property cycle peak", "have property prices peaked"
**Hook:** "If you're a homeowner wondering whether you've already captured most of the capital gain in your area — or whether there's more to come — this video breaks down exactly how to figure that out."
**Content:**
- What "capturing the gain" actually means (purchase price → current value → growth trajectory)
- How to read the cycle: are you on the upswing, plateau, or early decline?
- Suburb-specific data: "In Robina, the median has grown X% in 3 years. Here's what the leading indicators say about the next 12 months."
- The honest answer: "Nobody can tell you the exact peak. But here's how to know if you're close."
- Reserved insight at 6-7 min: the leading indicator that historically predicts the turn (wages, interest rates, supply pipeline)

### "Are Markets About to Spike in My Area? Will I Miss Out If I Sell Now?"

**Target query:** "property market spike 2026", "gold coast property going up", "should i wait to sell"
**Hook:** "The fear of selling too early — leaving money on the table — is one of the biggest anxieties homeowners have. Let's look at what the data actually says about whether your suburb is about to spike."
**Content:**
- What causes price spikes (supply constraint + demand surge + low days-on-market)
- Current leading indicators for each suburb
- Historical: when did the last spike happen, what triggered it, how long did it last?
- The trade-off: "Even if prices rise another 5%, does the cost of waiting (mortgage, opportunity cost, market risk) make it worth it?"

### "What's the Best Method of Sale for My Home?"

**Target query:** "auction vs private treaty", "best way to sell house", "should i auction my house"
**Hook:** "Auction, private treaty, expressions of interest, off-market — which one actually gets you the best price? I analysed the data."
**Content:**
- We already have the article "Auction vs Private Treaty: What the Data Actually Says"
- Break down by suburb: "In Robina, auction clearance rates are X%. In Burleigh, private treaty outsells auction by Y%."
- Method-by-market-condition: "In a hot market, auction. In a cool market, private treaty. Here's why."
- Reserved insight: the one method that consistently underperforms (data reveals)

### "Is It Currently a Buyers Market or Sellers Market?"

**Target query:** "buyers market or sellers market", "gold coast buyers market", "sellers market 2026"
**Hook:** "Everyone talks about buyers markets and sellers markets. But what does that actually mean, and which one are we in right now on the Gold Coast?"
**Content:**
- Define: what makes it a buyer's market vs seller's market (DOM, stock levels, auction clearance, price trends)
- Current Gold Coast data across all indicators
- Suburb-level breakdown: "Burleigh is a seller's market. Robina is balanced. Varsity is tilting towards buyers."
- What this means practically: "If you're buying in a seller's market, here's how to compete. If you're selling in a buyer's market, here's how to stand out."

### "What Do I Need to Do Before Deciding to Sell?"

**Target query:** "preparing to sell house", "things to do before selling", "should i renovate before selling"
**Hook:** "Before you even think about calling an agent, there are 5 things you need to figure out — and one of them might change your mind about selling entirely."
**Content:**
1. **Seasonal timing** — when do homes in your suburb sell fastest and for the most? Data-backed.
2. **Home preparation** — what's worth fixing vs what's a waste of money. "The $5K kitchen refresh that adds $30K vs the $50K renovation that adds $40K."
3. **Where are you moving to?** This is the big one: "If you're downsizing into a unit, units are performing well right now in X suburbs — which means your net cost of moving might be higher than you think. Or lower."
4. **Financial cost-benefit** — selling costs (agent fees, marketing, legal, moving) vs gain. "You need to sell for at least $X more than you bought for to break even after costs."
5. **The one question most people skip:** "What happens if you DON'T sell? What does the next 2 years look like if you stay?"
- Reserved insight at 6-7 min: the seasonal data — which months historically get the highest prices in each suburb

## High-Performing Facebook Crossover

Content that already works on Facebook → expand into long-form YouTube.

### "Someone Bought This Home X Months Ago. Now It's Back."

**Facebook performance:** Top-performing ad creative ($0.16/LPV)
**YouTube version:** 8-10 min deep dive. Why was it re-listed? What changed? Did they make money or lose money? What does this tell us about the market?
**Series potential:** One of these per month. Every re-listed property is a story.

### "What Buyers Actually Paid vs Asking Price"

**Facebook format:** Short data post with engagement
**YouTube version:** 10 min analysis. Pull last month's sales data. Show the gap between asking and selling. Breakdown by suburb, property type, price range. "In Robina, buyers paid an average of 3.2% below asking. In Burleigh, they paid 1.8% above."
**Series potential:** Monthly update.

### "Would You Pay $X for This?"

**Facebook format:** High-comment engagement post
**YouTube version:** 8 min video. Show 3-4 properties. Will gives his data-backed opinion on each. "The data says this is worth $1.15M. It's listed at $1.35M. Here's why the gap exists."
**Series potential:** Weekly or fortnightly. Always fresh content because new listings appear constantly.

### "Would You Rather: This or That?"

**Facebook format:** Side-by-side comparison, comment driver
**YouTube version:** 10 min deep comparison. Two properties at similar price, totally different trade-offs. Walk through both (on-location if possible). Pull valuation data, suburb data, lifestyle comparison. Let the viewer decide.
**Series potential:** Fortnightly. Endless supply of interesting matchups from our valuation data.

## Notes on Production

### B-Roll Shot Lists (Batch Film Per Suburb)

One 1-hour B-roll session per suburb covers multiple videos:

**Robina B-roll session:**
- Driving down main roads (Robina Parkway, Robina Town Centre Dr)
- Town Centre exterior + interior walking shots
- Lake/canal footage
- Robina State High School frontage
- Robina train station
- Parks (Robina Common, surrounding green spaces)
- Residential street footage (different price brackets: units near station, houses near lake, premium canal-front)
- Cafe/restaurant areas

**Use across:** "Living in Robina", "Is Now a Good Time to Buy in Robina", "Should I Sell in Robina", any Robina property story. One filming session serves 4+ videos.

### Script-to-Data Pipeline

For every video, AI provides verified data from the pipeline:
- Median prices (exact, with source date)
- Sales volumes (quarterly comparison)
- Days on market (current vs 3-month ago)
- Price trends (12-month trajectory)
- Comparable sales for any specific property
- Valuation vs listing price gap
- All pulled from `Gold_Coast` database collections, verified against pipeline enrichment
