## New Ad Creation Pipeline

**Weekly cadence (starting Sprint 1):**

1. AI identifies best-performing ad format from current data
2. AI finds 2-3 new properties or angles from pipeline that fit the winning format
3. AI drafts ad creative (copy + image selection)
4. Will reviews and approves (5 min per ad)
5. AI creates ads in Facebook Ads Manager
6. Ads go live with budget from paused underperformers

**Target: 2-3 new ad variants per week**, continuously testing and replacing. Bottom performer each week gets paused, budget shifts to winner.
