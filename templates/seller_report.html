How We Calculate Your Valuation

Unlike automated estimates, our valuation selects comparable properties that closely match yours in size, configuration, and location. Each comparable is individually adjusted for differences in floor area, land size, condition, and location premium. The final figure is a weighted average of these adjusted values — giving greater weight to properties that are most similar, most recent, and have the best data quality.
