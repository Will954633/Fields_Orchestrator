# "Before You List" Book — Packaging Research (2026-07-23)

## Book dimensions (confirmed from the actual print files)
- Trim size: **7" × 11"** (178mm × 279mm) — checked via `pdfinfo` on both press and digital PDFs.
- ~98-100 pages, so realistic bound thickness is likely 5-8mm depending on stock — worth confirming
  with whichever printer does the actual binding (Officeworks sample run, per Will's note).
- **Not a standard ISO size** (closest ISO neighbours are A4 210×297mm or the US "digest" trade size) —
  most off-the-shelf mailer size charts won't have an exact match; a wrap-style mailer (adjustable,
  folds around the item) sidesteps this better than a rigid pre-sized box.

## Material: cardboard vs compostable plastic
**Recommend cardboard (recycled/recyclable corrugated), not compostable plastic**, for two concrete
reasons found in research: compostable mailers run ~40% more expensive, AND compostable packaging
isn't widely composted in Australia yet (no broad household/council composting stream for it) — so
the environmental benefit doesn't actually land the way it would with a mature composting system.
Recycled cardboard is both cheaper and the more genuinely sustainable choice right now, not a
compromise.
