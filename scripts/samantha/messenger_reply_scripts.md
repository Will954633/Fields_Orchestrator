# Messenger Reply Scripts + Escalation Cheat-Sheet (v1)

**For:** Will, answering replies to the live click-to-Messenger ad (campaign 120251469051360134).
**Status:** DRAFT for Will. Samantha drafts; Will sends (lead outreach stays human-sent).
**Rules baked in:** no advice ("you should…"), no forecasts, no single valuation figure, no forbidden words, disclose the digital assistant if Samantha ever answers. One soft CTA. Stay inside Meta's 24-hour window — reply promptly.

**The goal of every conversation:** consented chat → give real suburb/street data value → offer to build their exact-address mini-site → if warm, offer a call from Will / hand-deliver the *Before You List* book → listing appointment.

---

## 0. Welcome / Instant-reply (set manually in Business Suite Inbox — can't be set via API)
> "Hi, thanks for reaching out — this is Fields, we build free, data-only market snapshots for Robina, Burleigh Waters and Varsity Lakes. Happy to put one together for you. Which suburb is your home in?"

## 1. They tapped an ice-breaker / said their suburb
> "Great — [Suburb] is one of our core patches, so I've got solid data on it. Want the general suburb snapshot (recent nearby sales, how long homes are taking to sell, current buyer demand), or a report built for your exact address?"

## 2. They want the exact-address report
> "Perfect. What's the street address? I'll build a data report for your specific home — comparable sales near you, days on market for homes like yours, and the buyer demand we're seeing in your pocket of [Suburb]. No cost, no obligation."
- → hand the address to Samantha: generate + VERIFY the mini-site renders before sending the link. Core suburbs only (Robina / Varsity Lakes / Burleigh Waters).

## 3. Sending the mini-site
> "Here's your home's data report: [mini-site link]. It's all sourced data — recent comparable sales, days on market, and demand for your area, shown as ranges rather than one headline number. Have a look and tell me what stands out."

## 4. Soft qualifier (no pressure, no advice)
> "Out of interest — are you mostly keeping an eye on the market, or is selling something you're weighing up over the next 6–12 months? Either's fine, it just helps me tailor what I send."

## 5. Warm → the one CTA
> "If it's useful, Will (our founder) is happy to walk you through your report on a quick 10-minute call — no obligation. Want me to grab a time? We can also drop a copy of our *Before You List* guide to your door."

## 6. Not ready / just browsing
> "No problem at all — the snapshot's yours to keep. I'll leave it there; reach out any time you want it refreshed or want the numbers on a specific home."

## Escalation cheat-sheet — hand to Will immediately when the lead:
- gives a **real address in a core suburb** and wants a report (build + verify mini-site first),
- asks **"what's my home worth?"** → do NOT give a figure in chat; "I'll show you the comparable range in your report rather than a single number," then send the mini-site,
- says they're **thinking of selling / talking to agents** → warm, offer the call,
- asks anything requiring **advice or a legal/financial opinion** → "I'll have Will come back to you on that" (don't answer),
- is **outside the 3 core suburbs** → be honest: "We're deepest on Robina, Burleigh Waters and Varsity Lakes right now — I can still share general Gold Coast data."

## Do NOT
- quote a single valuation figure in chat (ranges / "in your report" only),
- tell them what they should do (no advice, no "now's a good time"),
- forecast prices,
- send a mini-site link that hasn't been generated + visually verified for that exact address.
