# Letter — Analyse-Your-Home address-only follow-up (physical mail)

**Status:** DRAFT ONLY. Will posts / hand-delivers. Do NOT send.
**Pairs with:** the physical *Before You List* book (`08_Seller-Book/`).
**Audience:** the ~10 people who entered ONLY an address on `/analyse-your-home`
(no name, no email, no phone). They gave us the address by typing it, so a **posted
letter/book is compliant** — it is not Spam-Act / DNC gated. This is the only
compliant way to reach these entries.
**Use for CORE-suburb addresses first** (Robina, Varsity Lakes, Burleigh Waters) —
those are the ones where we can generate + verify a real home mini-site to back the
claims. Non-core (Carrara, Tallebudgera, Murwillumbah NSW, Merrimac, Mudgeeraba)
get the letter + book only, no mini-site, until the generator covers them.

**Personalise before printing:** replace `[STREET]` and `[SUBURB]`.
**Pre-send practice (Will, 2026-07-15):** for core-suburb addresses, generate AND
verify the home mini-site renders correctly BEFORE the letter goes out, so every
claim in it is backed by a real, working report.

**Editorial check:** no advice, no forecast, no single valuation figure (comparable
sale prices are exact transaction facts, not an estimate of their home); no forbidden
words; one soft CTA; suburbs capitalised.
