## [Q1-REPORT-LOGO-FLEXSTRETCH] Cover logo distorted by flexbox stretch — 11:52 AEST
**Symptom:** Cover logo rendered oversized and stretched horizontally — width spanned full content area despite `width: auto` on the img. Image height proportional to that stretched width, ~52mm tall instead of the intended 18mm. Looked distorted and dominated the cover.
**Root cause:** Cover `<div class="page">` is `display: flex; flex-direction: column`. Flex items default to `align-self: stretch` along the cross-axis (width, in a column flex). That stretching forced the image to fill 100% of the content width, overriding `width: auto`. Browser then scaled height up to maintain aspect ratio.
**Fix:** Added `align-self: flex-start` to the img inline style so it opts out of cross-axis stretching. Also reduced height from 18mm → 14mm and bottom margin from 14mm → 12mm for a more refined masthead size.
**Files:** `10_Market_Report/pipeline/templates/report.html`
**Recurrence:** First — but the pattern (flex-stretch overriding `width: auto` on img) is a generic gotcha. Worth knowing for any future flex layouts.
