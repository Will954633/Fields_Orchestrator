## [FLOORPLAN-DEBRAND-V3.1] Content-aware removal (graphic logos + coloured banners) — built today
**Symptom:** v3 detected stylized/graphic logos but removal was incomplete — solid coloured banners (realtyblue blue bar), grey low-contrast wordmarks (TMG "Talent Mindset Growth", BANGO STERA, SPROULE) survived: stroke-inpaint only erases dark ink and left coloured/grey fills.
**Root cause:** One-size removal. Stroke-inpaint can't clear solid coloured fills or low-contrast grey logos; flatten-to-paper would destroy coloured ROOMS if applied in the plan body. Also vision-LLM (Claude) boxes are spatially loose — they clip the logo (tagline missed) or over-cover a region.
**Fix:** Rewrote `remove_branding` as a content+location-aware router. (1) Page background sampled; non-paper mask = pixels differing from paper by colour OR luminance. (2) Coloured/grey graphic in a box -> grow to the full contiguous same-colour region via connected components (a partial banner box -> the whole bar), accepted ONLY if a wide thin strip / outer margin / small blob (a coloured ROOM is blocky+body -> rejected). (3) Branding box in the OUTER MARGIN -> flatten wholesale (clears grey wordmarks that defeat inpaint), first grown to the real ink extent (`_grow_flatten_box`: connected-components in a bounded window, self-limits at paper gaps so it can't jump a white gap into the wall network). (4) Box in the DRAWING BODY -> stroke-inpaint, but ONLY if sparse (non-paper frac <0.30); a dense body box is the model loosely boxing a filled region -> SKIP, so a watermark's loose box can't strip a room's walls/label into a blob (the tight Vision text box still removes the actual watermark). Net safety invariant: nothing over the plan body is ever flattened; dense body boxes are skipped -> coloured rooms always survive.
**Result (50 plans, 0 failures):** realtyblue full-width banner GONE; TMG logo + tagline GONE; coloured/dark margin logos (Digi360, @realty) GONE. Plan 42 Floor 2 fully intact (all bedrooms/bath/WIC/sitting room reconstructed, blue fills preserved, no blob). Verified no plan-body destruction across the 12 riskiest (coloured-room) plans + heavy-removal plans (BoxBrownie 440m², Pure Design/Plans For Purpose) — rooms, labels, dims, addresses, areas all preserved. 35/50 plans Claude-caught (111 boxes). Known residuals (acceptable for POC): a logo whose Claude box is mislocated survives (BANGO STERA — box didn't overlap the logo); occasional faint top-corner mark.
