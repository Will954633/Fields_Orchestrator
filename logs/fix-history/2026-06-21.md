## [APPRAISAL-COMPLIANCE] Statutory CMA on the "your home" digital appraisal — 20:35 AEST

**Symptom:** The "your home" mini-site is a digital appraisal but did not surface a Property Occupations Act 2014 (Qld) s 215 / Sch 2 Comparative Market Analysis (≥3 sales within the previous 6 months, similar standard, within 5 km). The valuation engine selects comps suburb-first over ~12 months and never proved the statutory minimum; UI copy also misstated the window as "18/24 months". (Audit item A — see `09_Appraisals/your-home-minisite-compliance-audit-2026-06-21.md`.)

**Root cause:** No statutory-CMA selection layer existed. The engine's comp set is a sound estimate but is not, on its face, the Sch 2 CMA. Data analysis (3,120 fresh Gold_Coast sold records) showed the worry about the 6 mo/5 km rule starving comps was unfounded: core suburbs get 100% coverage with 35–62 qualifying sales each (never need the cross-suburb ring); the 5 km ring actually *raises* thin-suburb coverage from 72%→98%. Restricting to 6 months (vs 12) nudges the raw comp level only ~+2–4% in core suburbs (engine time-adjustment absorbs most of it). The naive "broaden everyone to 5 km cross-suburb" approach was rejected (Varsity Lakes +12.9%, geographic drift).

**Fix:** New `scripts/property_reports/statutory_cma.py` builds the CMA suburb-first (≥3 sold / 6 mo / 5 km / same type / beds ±1), reaching the 5 km cross-suburb ring only when a suburb is short; if still <3, flags the s 215 written-explanation fallback. Drawn ONLY from fresh, coordinate-bearing Gold_Coast sold (the legacy Target_Market source is stale/coordless). **Display + light support only** — does NOT re-base the engine math (cross-suburb comps need location-adjustment + backtest first). Wired into `slot_resolver.resolve_all` (`valuation.statutory_cma` slot). Frontend: new `StatutoryCMA.tsx` rendered under the headline range in `ValuationTab`, with the mandatory "as at / valid until" stamp (6-month window means the CMA expires). Backfilled all 18 live reports (all compliant, basis=suburb). Verified live on `/your-home/20-silkyoak-court-burleigh-waters` (61 comps, rendered, 0 network errors) + tsc clean.

**Files:** `scripts/property_reports/statutory_cma.py` (new), `scripts/property_reports/slot_resolver.py`; website `src/pages/YourHomePage/components/StatutoryCMA.tsx` (new), `tabs/ValuationTab.tsx`, `data/homeFixture.ts`, `YourHomePage.tsx`. Commits: orchestrator 5c26ba2 + a21b989; website 0d47a4c.
