## [MINISITE-MOBILE-OVERFLOW] SoftCta flexShrink:0 broke mobile layout on 7 of 9 mini-site tabs — 20:25 AEST
**Symptom:** At mobile widths the mini-site (`/your-home/<slug>`) scrolled horizontally — page scrollWidth pinned at 516px regardless of viewport (375/360/320px), content left-shifted with a dead right gutter, and the off-canvas Complete Data Record drawer bled into view.
