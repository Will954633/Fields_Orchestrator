## [TRACKING-001] Email tracking server with web PDF viewer — 13:27 AEST
**Symptom:** No way to track if leads open emails or engage with property reports.
**Root cause:** No tracking infrastructure existed.
**Fix:** Built full email tracking system:
- Flask server on port 3051 (systemd: fields-tracking, nginx: /track/)
