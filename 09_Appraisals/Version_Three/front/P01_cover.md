# Page 01 — Cover

**Format:** Full-bleed cover. Right-hand page (front of document).

Inherits the V2 cover layout — it is one of the strongest single pages V2 produced. V3 makes only minor adjustments: tighter typography hierarchy, removal of "Prepared for Dee" subtitle (moved to closing note for emotional weight), and standardisation of the cover footer.

## Designer notes

- **Image:** carry forward V2 cover image (`assets/hero_0.png` or equivalent) — the twilight pool exterior with bushland edge and glass balustrade. This image carries enormous emotional weight and is the single best photograph in the project.
- **F watermark, top-left:** keep the large semi-transparent copper F that V2 used. It anchors the brand without competing with the property.
- **Typography:**
  - "13 Terrace Court" — large display serif, sand/cream colour, tight leading (about 0.95)
  - "MERRIMAC, QLD 4226" — small caps, slightly tracked, copper or warm grey
- **Removed from V2 cover:** "Prepared for Dee" line. The recipient's name is reserved for the closing note where it lands as personal commitment, not a label.
- **Bleed:** image runs to 100% bleed at top and sides; cream band at bottom is approximately 18% of page height.

---

## What this page must achieve

The cover must do one job: make the seller want to open the document.

The combination of:
- a beautiful twilight image of *someone else's home like theirs*,
- a serif treatment of the address that elevates it to magazine-cover scale,
- and the single line `PROPERTY POSITION REPORT` (a category they've never seen before)

…should produce the first emotion of the journey: **curiosity.** "What is this?"
