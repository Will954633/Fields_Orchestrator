## Designer notes

- **The four numbered steps use copper numerals** matching the spread-number treatment from the interior spreads. Visual continuity.
- **Step formatting:** number + em-dash + short title on one line (cap-serif, 14pt); description below (warm-grey body, 12pt, 1.5 line-height). Each step takes about 18mm of vertical space.
- **The CTA is soft and specific.** "If you'd like to proceed, the next step is a 45-minute walkthrough" — gives a clear, low-friction action with a defined time commitment. It does not say "to engage Fields, please…" or "to book us, contact…". Honour the soft-CTA preference (per CLAUDE.md editorial guidance).
- **Contact block is plain.** No "Connect with us on LinkedIn." No social handles. No QR codes. The seller has Will's email; that is sufficient.
- **No image.** No callout box. No "Why Fields" panel. Operational pages stay operational.

---

## What this page must achieve

The seller must finish this page able to take action without further explanation.

If they are interested, they email Will or reply to the appraisal delivery. If they are not yet ready, the page does not push.

The four-step ladder also reframes "engaging Fields" from a binary commitment ("hire us") to a low-stakes inspection ("a 45-minute walkthrough"). That reframing reduces friction at the most important moment in the seller journey.
