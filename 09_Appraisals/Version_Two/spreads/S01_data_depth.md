### Headline
> **We see your home in more dimensions than any other agency in Australia.**

### Lede

Most agencies look at three things: bedrooms, bathrooms, lot size. We process every photograph, every centimetre of the floor plan, every metre of the satellite image, every street-view frame, and every named point of interest within walking distance. Then we cross-reference what we find against thousands of recent sales and every active listing in the southern Gold Coast.

### What Fields uniquely does

- **Floor plan analysis.** Every room dimensioned, layout flow scored, dual-living potential mapped, indoor-to-outdoor area ratios computed. Most agents work from a PNG; we work from the structured data underneath it.

- **Photo-by-photo condition scoring.** Each interior photograph passed through a multimodal vision model (GPT-4V): kitchen condition, bathroom finish, materials called by name, dated-vs-renovated assessed at the room level rather than the property level.

- **Satellite analysis.** Orientation, neighbour proximity, tree canopy coverage, pool envelope, north-facing yard fraction, bushland-vs-arterial-road adjacency. The view from above is the first view a serious buyer takes — we frame it before they see it.

- **Street-view scoring.** Front presentation, set-back, kerb appeal, traffic-noise risk, visible road network. The first-five-second impression that decides whether a buyer opens the floor plan.

- **POI walking-distance ledger.** Every school, park, cafe, supermarket, and public-transport stop within 1.5 km, measured by walking route — not the Euclidean shortcut every other proximity tool uses.

- **Combinatorial comparable matching.** Once your home's attributes are extracted, we run them as a query against ~270 active listings and ~2,000 sold transactions to surface what is rare, what is in demand, and what has historically commanded a premium.

### The evidence

Across the southern Gold Coast we hold approximately **40,000 cadastral records, 270 active listings, and 2,075 sold transactions**, refreshed nightly. Every active property is run through our analysis pipeline as it appears on the market. **No other agency operating in our suburbs maintains this layer of data on every property.**
