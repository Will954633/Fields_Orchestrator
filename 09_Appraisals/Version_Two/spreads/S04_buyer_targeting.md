### Lede

A premium price requires a premium buyer in the room. Most agencies hope the right buyer will see the listing on Domain. We engineer the campaign to put the home in front of every buyer matching the personas identified on Spread 03, on the channels each persona actually uses, at the moments they are most receptive.

### What Fields uniquely does

- **Buyer-pool sizing per persona, per price band.** Using realestate.com.au monthly search-volume data for the suburb-and-bracket combination, plus our own engagement telemetry across the Fields ecosystem, we can estimate how many qualified buyers exist for your home this month — not as a vague range, but as a specific number with a confidence band.

- **Channel mix designed around persona behaviour, not agency convention.** The Established Owner-Occupier With Capital reads Domain's curated newsletters; the Relocating Sydney Family browses LinkedIn during work hours; the Multi-Generational Family searches Facebook Marketplace on Sunday evenings. The campaign is engineered around each, not against a single agency template.

- **Custom-audience targeting at the platform level.** We maintain Facebook Custom Audiences specific to the southern Gold Coast (Page Engagers 180d, Robina/VL/Merrimac Buyers, Seller Intent, GC Market Interest, All Ad Engagers 180d), plus lookalike audiences modelled on existing Fields enquirers. Activated for your campaign on day one.

- **Geo-temporal layering.** Buyer behaviour is time-of-day specific: 7-9pm weeknights for browsing, 10am-noon Saturday for inspection-planning, 4-6pm Sunday for decision conversations. Ad delivery is layered to match.

- **Engagement telemetry feeds back to the campaign.** PostHog tracks every interaction with the listing. Within 72 hours of going live, we know which photo has the highest dwell time, which portion of the editorial copy is being re-read, and which feature is driving the most enquiries — and we adjust the campaign accordingly.

- **Buyer database outreach pre-launch.** Before the public listing goes live, qualified buyers we have engaged through the broader Fields ecosystem (newsletter, Five Property Friday, market intelligence pages, decision feed) are notified and matched to your property by archetype, price band, and feature requirement. This is the practice known in the industry as the [Mat Steinwede method](https://fieldsestate.com.au/articles/699d7223a47edd0001e077e8) — by the time the first public open home runs, serious buyers are already competing.
