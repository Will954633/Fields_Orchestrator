### What the telemetry enables (the feedback loop)

1. **Within 72h of launch:** identify which photo holds the longest dwell time per visitor → swap that photo into the lead position on the FB ad creative + the Domain primary photo.
2. **Within 72h:** identify which paragraph of the editorial gets the highest re-read rate → reuse as the FB ad headline copy.
3. **Within 7 days:** identify which Google Search keyword is converting at the highest rate → reallocate Google Ads budget away from underperforming keywords.
4. **Within 14 days:** identify which retargeting trigger (pool photo, bushland photo, school proximity copy, etc.) is producing the highest deep-engagement rate → build a follow-up creative around it.
