## Cover specification

**Hero photograph:** twilight exterior of the property (outsourced if not on file). Edge-bleed, four-process print on coated stock for print edition. Single colour-graded master used across all three editions (Digital Quick-Read, Print Master, Living Microsite).

**Logomark:** Fields "F" mark, top-right, in white over the hero image.

**Address block:** large copper panel, lower-left, address set in the brand serif (60-80pt). Suburb / postcode line beneath in a smaller weight.

**Below-the-fold caption:**
- Section label: "PROPERTY POSITION REPORT" (small, all-caps, copper)
- "Prepared for [Client Name]" (heading weight)
- "by Will Simpson, Lead Analyst"
- "fieldsestate.com.au"

**Date:** [Send date], precise day-month-year, lower-left.

**Tagline:** "SMARTER WITH DATA" with the Fields F-mark, lower-right.

**Spec mark / report ID:** Inherits the V1 design — small, lower-corner. Reinforces the "this is yours alone" feeling.

## Print-edition only

- Embossed Fields logomark on copper foil.
- Heavy uncoated stock for the cover, lay-flat binding.

## Microsite-edition only

- 6-8 second cover loop — twilight pan of the property — autoplay muted, then pause on still.
