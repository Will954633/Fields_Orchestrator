# Back · What Happens Next

A single page. Three steps. No closing pitch.

The page does not ask for the listing. It defines the next conversation, and explicitly preserves the seller's right to walk away with the report.

## Editorial rules

- **Three steps, no fourth.** The third step is the explicit invitation to walk away. Adding a fourth step ("Sign with us") would damage the integrity of the third.
- **The book-a-conversation link is a soft default, not a hard call.** "Book at:" is set in the same weight as "Or write to:" — the seller has two equivalent paths.
- **The "this report is yours to keep" language is non-negotiable.** It is the structural promise that distinguishes Fields' approach from a marketing-tool appraisal.
- **No commission discussion. No service-tier pricing. No team page.** Those belong in the conversation, not the report.

---

## Print-edition only

A tipped-in business card on the inside back cover. Heavy weight, foil-blocked. The kind of object the seller keeps in their drawer.
