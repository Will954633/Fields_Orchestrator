## Editorial rules

- **The verdict page restates conclusions, not arguments.** Anyone reading this page who wants the working has the spread reference cited inline.
- **The price ranges shown here must match the price ranges shown on Spread 07.** Any divergence between this page and the underlying spread is a defect.
- **No marketing claims.** Not "the best home in Merrimac." Not "exceptional value." Numbers and named features only.
- **The page reads in 30 seconds.** That is the design target. The skim-reader who arrives at this page and reads nothing else should walk away with the price range, the method, and the timing decision in their head.

---

## What changed in V2

V1 surfaced the verdict on page 6-7 ("The Fields Take") near the front of the report, as the lead claim. V2 places the verdict at the back, after ten spreads have built the argument up. The reader arrives at the verdict having earned it. The verdict page is also the single page most likely to be photographed, screenshotted, or shared — placing it last gives the reader the full document's weight to bring with it.
